# AI Search Engine Optimization (AI SEO) - Complete Guide

## 🤖 What is AI SEO?

AI SEO optimizes your website for AI-powered search engines and chatbots like:
- **ChatGPT** (OpenAI)
- **Claude** (Anthropic)
- **Perplexity AI**
- **Google Bard/Gemini**
- **Bing Chat** (Microsoft Copilot)
- **SearchGPT**

These AI systems crawl websites and use the information to answer user queries directly.

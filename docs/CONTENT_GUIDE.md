## Content Guidelines

### Tone of Voice
- **Professional**: Convey expertise and reliability
- **Friendly**: Warm and welcoming, not corporate
- **Clear**: Simple language, easy to understand
- **Helpful**: Anticipate questions, provide answers
- **Local**: Show knowledge of Poreč and Istria

### Writing Style
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Bullet points for lists
- Active voice
- Clear headings and subheadings
- Scannable content
- Mobile-friendly formatting

### Keywords Integration
Naturally incorporate keywords:
- Taxi Poreč
- Taxi service Poreč
- Airport transfer Poreč
- Pula Airport to Poreč
- Istria taxi
- Reliable taxi Poreč
- 24/7 taxi service

### Call-to-Action Language
- "Call Now"
- "Book Your Ride"
- "Get a Quote"
- "Message Us on WhatsApp"
- "Contact Us Today"
- "Reserve Your Transfer"

### Trust Signals
- Years of experience
- Licensed and insured
- Professional drivers
- Modern fleet
- 24/7 availability
- Transparent pricing
- Customer testimonials (if available)

## Multilingual Content

### Croatian Version
- Primary language

### English Version
- Secondary language

## SEO Content Requirements

### Meta Information
**Title**: 50-60 characters, include primary keyword
**Description**: 150-160 characters, compelling CTA

### Header Hierarchy
- One H1 per page
- Logical H2, H3 structure
- Include keywords in headers

### Alt Text for Images
- Descriptive and keyword-rich
- Example: "Professional taxi vehicle in Poreč old town"

### Internal Linking
- Link to relevant sections
- Descriptive anchor text
- Logical navigation flow
